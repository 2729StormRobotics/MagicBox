Complicated, requires external board if screen used
- Motors
  - Drive each = 4 potentiometers (AIN)
  - Drive direction = 4 switches (GPIO)
  - Lock pairs = 2 switches (GPIO)
- Pneumatics
  - Fire each = 4 switches (GPIO)
  - Indicate each = 4 LEDS (CPU?)
- General
  - Screen (HERO Port 8)
  - Run = 1 switch (GPIO)
- Totals
  - 4 Analog in
  - 11 Digital in
  - 4 CPU
  - 1 Screen
